Gen 3 Over-used

This is my favorite metagame of competetive Pokemon, I find the elements and counterpicks of this metagame to be extremely fun.

The top threats in this metagame include

  • Tyranitar,
  • Zapdos,
  • Metagross,
  • Skarmory,
  • and Blissey.

Gen 3 OU stands out in particular as a Spikes metagame, in which the Spikes entry hazard is one of the dominant forces upon gameplay. Spikes dissincentivizes switch-stalling and greatly alters the matagame. Additionally as a permanent weather metagame, sandstorm stays up indefinitely, making Tyranitar potentially the most dominant pokemon of this metagame.
